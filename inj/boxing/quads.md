## Squat with Guard Position
- **IT**: Squat in Posizione di Guardia
- **Difficulty**: beginner
- **Mechanics**: compound
- **Force**: push
- **Unilateral**: false
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: low
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Rectus Femoris, Vastus Lateralis, Vastus Medialis
- **Secondary Muscles**: Gluteus Maximus, Hamstrings, Erector Spinae
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Tags**: boxing, squat, guard-position, conditioning, lower-body

**Variants:**
- Weighted Squat Guard | Squat con Guardia e Peso | diff: intermediate
- Pause Squat Guard | Squat con Pausa in Guardia | diff: intermediate
