## Thoracic CARs (Controlled Articular Rotations)
- **IT**: Rotazioni Articolari Controllate Toraciche
- **Difficulty**: intermediate
- **Mechanics**: isolation
- **Force**: dynamic
- **Unilateral**: false
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: low
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Thoracic Erector Spinae, Multifidus Thoracic
- **Secondary Muscles**: Rhomboid Major, Intercostals, Serratus Anterior
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Tags**: mobility, back, thoracic, cars, dynamic, intermediate, recovery
- **Duration**: 3-5 reps per direction

**Variants:**
- Thoracic CARs Seated | CARs Toracici da Seduto | diff: beginner
- Thoracic CARs in Quadruped | CARs Toracici in Quadrupedia | diff: intermediate
