## Hip CARs (Controlled Articular Rotations)
- **IT**: Rotazioni Articolari Controllate dell'Anca
- **Difficulty**: intermediate
- **Mechanics**: isolation
- **Force**: dynamic
- **Unilateral**: true
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: low
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Psoas Major, Iliacus, Rectus Femoris
- **Secondary Muscles**: Gluteus Maximus, Piriformis, Tensor Fasciae Latae, Hip Adductors
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Tags**: mobility, hip_flexors, cars, dynamic, intermediate, recovery
- **Duration**: 3-5 reps per direction per side, slow and controlled

**Variants:**
- Hip CARs Supine | CARs Anca Supino | diff: beginner
- Hip CARs at Wall | CARs Anca al Muro | diff: beginner
