## Shoulder CARs (Controlled Articular Rotations)
- **IT**: Rotazioni Articolari Controllate della Spalla
- **Difficulty**: intermediate
- **Mechanics**: isolation
- **Force**: dynamic
- **Unilateral**: true
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: low
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Deltoid - Anterior, Deltoid - Lateral, Deltoid - Posterior, Supraspinatus
- **Secondary Muscles**: Infraspinatus, Teres Minor, Subscapularis, Serratus Anterior
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Tags**: mobility, shoulders, cars, dynamic, intermediate, recovery
- **Duration**: 3-5 reps per direction per side, slow and controlled

**Variants:**
- Shoulder CARs at Wall | CARs Spalla al Muro | diff: beginner
- Shoulder CARs Supine | CARs Spalla Supino | diff: beginner
