## Pigeon Pose (Eka Pada Rajakapotasana)
- **IT**: Posizione del Piccione
- **Difficulty**: intermediate
- **Mechanics**: compound
- **Force**: static
- **Unilateral**: true
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: medium
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Piriformis, Gluteus Maximus, Gluteus Medius
- **Secondary Muscles**: Psoas Major, Iliacus, Obturator Internus
- **Equipment**: Yoga Mat
- **Tags**: yoga, glutes, hips, hip opener, intermediate

- King Pigeon | Piccione Reale (Raja Kapotasana) | diff: elite | spot: false
