## Pigeon Pose
- **IT**: Posizione del Piccione
- **Difficulty**: intermediate
- **Mechanics**: isolation
- **Force**: static
- **Unilateral**: true
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: medium
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Piriformis, Gluteus Maximus, Obturator Internus
- **Secondary Muscles**: Gemellus Superior, Gemellus Inferior, Hip External Rotators
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Tags**: stretching, glutes, static, intermediate, recovery, yoga
- **Duration**: 60-120 sec hold per side

**Variants:**
- Reclined Pigeon | Piccione Supino | diff: beginner
- Double Pigeon | Doppio Piccione | diff: advanced
- Sleeping Pigeon | Piccione Addormentato | diff: intermediate
