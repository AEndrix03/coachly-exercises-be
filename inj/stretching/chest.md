## Doorway Chest Stretch
- **IT**: Allungamento Pettorali al Stipite della Porta
- **Difficulty**: beginner
- **Mechanics**: isolation
- **Force**: static
- **Unilateral**: false
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: low
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Pectoralis Major - Mid Sternal Head, Pectoralis Major - Upper Clavicular Head
- **Secondary Muscles**: Anterior Deltoid, Pectoralis Minor, Serratus Anterior
- **Equipment**: Doorframe
- **Tags**: stretching, chest, static, beginner, recovery
- **Duration**: 30-60 sec hold

**Variants:**
- High Doorway Chest Stretch | Allungamento Alto alla Porta | diff: beginner
- Low Doorway Chest Stretch | Allungamento Basso alla Porta | diff: beginner
- Single Arm Doorway Stretch | Allungamento Monobraccio alla Porta | diff: beginner
