## Standing Quad Stretch
- **IT**: Allungamento Quadricipiti in Piedi
- **Difficulty**: beginner
- **Mechanics**: isolation
- **Force**: static
- **Unilateral**: true
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: low
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Rectus Femoris, Vastus Lateralis, Vastus Medialis
- **Secondary Muscles**: Tensor Fasciae Latae, Sartorius
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Tags**: stretching, quads, static, beginner, recovery
- **Duration**: 30-60 sec hold per side

**Variants:**
- Standing Quad Stretch with Wall Support | Quadricipiti in Piedi con Appoggio | diff: beginner
- Standing Quad Stretch with Band | Quadricipiti in Piedi con Elastico | diff: beginner
