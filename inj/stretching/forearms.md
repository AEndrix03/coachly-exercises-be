## Wrist Flexor Stretch
- **IT**: Allungamento dei Flessori del Polso
- **Difficulty**: beginner
- **Mechanics**: isolation
- **Force**: static
- **Unilateral**: true
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: low
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Flexor Carpi Radialis, Flexor Carpi Ulnaris, Flexor Digitorum Superficialis
- **Secondary Muscles**: Pronator Teres, Brachioradialis
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Tags**: stretching, forearms, wrists, static, beginner, recovery
- **Duration**: 30-60 sec hold per side

**Variants:**
- Wrist Flexor Stretch on Floor | Flessori Polso sul Pavimento | diff: beginner
- Wrist Flexor Stretch with Wall | Flessori Polso al Muro | diff: beginner
