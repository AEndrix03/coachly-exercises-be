## Butterfly Stretch
- **IT**: Stretching a Farfalla
- **Difficulty**: beginner
- **Mechanics**: isolation
- **Force**: static
- **Unilateral**: false
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: low
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Adductor Longus, Adductor Brevis, Gracilis
- **Secondary Muscles**: Pectineus, Sartorius, Hip Flexors
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Tags**: stretching, adductors, static, beginner, recovery
- **Duration**: 30-60 sec hold

**Variants:**
- Reclining Butterfly | Farfalla Supina | diff: beginner
- Weighted Butterfly | Farfalla con Peso | diff: intermediate
- Active Butterfly Press | Farfalla Attiva con Pressione | diff: beginner
