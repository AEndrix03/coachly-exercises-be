- **IT**: Allungamento Spalla Trasversale
- **Difficulty**: beginner
- **Mechanics**: isolation
- **Force**: static
- **Unilateral**: true

- **Primary Muscles**: Deltoid - Posterior, Infraspinatus, Teres Minor
- **Secondary Muscles**: Rhomboid Major, Trapezius - Middle
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Tags**: stretching, shoulders, static, beginner, recovery
- **Duration**: 30-60 sec hold per side
