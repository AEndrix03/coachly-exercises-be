## Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch
- **IT**: Allungamento Flessori Anca in Ginocchio
- **Difficulty**: beginner
- **Mechanics**: isolation
- **Force**: static
- **Unilateral**: true
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: low
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Psoas Major, Iliacus
- **Secondary Muscles**: Rectus Femoris, Tensor Fasciae Latae
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Tags**: stretching, hip_flexors, static, beginner, recovery
- **Duration**: 30-60 sec hold per side

- Kneeling Hip Flexor Stretch on Pad | Allungamento Flessori su Cuscino | diff: beginner
