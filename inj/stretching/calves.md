## Standing Calf Stretch (Wall)
- **IT**: Allungamento Polpacci in Piedi al Muro
- **Difficulty**: beginner
- **Mechanics**: isolation
- **Force**: static
- **Unilateral**: true
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: low
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Gastrocnemius - Medial Head, Gastrocnemius - Lateral Head
- **Secondary Muscles**: Soleus, Tibialis Posterior, Fibularis Longus
- **Equipment**: Wall
- **Tags**: stretching, calves, static, beginner, recovery
- **Duration**: 30-60 sec hold per side

**Variants:**
- Standing Calf Stretch with Foot on Wall | Polpacci al Muro con Piede | diff: beginner
- Standing Calf Stretch on Step | Polpacci sul Gradino | diff: beginner
