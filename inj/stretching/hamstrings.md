## Standing Hamstring Stretch
- **IT**: Allungamento Femorali in Piedi
- **Difficulty**: beginner
- **Mechanics**: isolation
- **Force**: static
- **Unilateral**: true
- **Bodyweight**: true
- **Risk**: low
- **Spotter**: false
- **Primary Muscles**: Biceps Femoris, Semitendinosus, Semimembranosus
- **Secondary Muscles**: Gluteus Maximus, Gastrocnemius
- **Equipment**: None (Bodyweight)
- **Tags**: stretching, hamstrings, static, beginner, recovery
- **Duration**: 30-60 sec hold per side

**Variants:**
- Standing Hamstring Stretch with Foot on Elevated Surface | Femorali in Piedi su Superficie Elevata | diff: intermediate
